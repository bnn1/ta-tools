## Key Observations

### Strengths
- **RSI**: Dominant across all sizes (2.6-3.9x faster)
- **MFI**: Massive wins (4.6-7.4x faster)
- **ADX**: Strong performance (2.6-4.8x faster)
- **Stochastic**: Consistent wins (2.0-2.3x faster)
- **StochRSI**: Excellent (3.5-4.3x faster)
- **Ichimoku**: Good margins (1.9-2.2x faster)

### Areas for Improvement
- **SMA**: Loses at small/big datasets, wins only at huge
- **Bollinger Bands**: Loses to indicatorts at all batch sizes
- **WMA**: Loses at small/big datasets
- **Streaming overhead**: Several indicators lose in streaming mode by small margins

### Root Cause Analysis
- WASM boundary crossing overhead hurts simple indicators (SMA, BBands) at small scales
- Streaming losses are marginal (1.01-1.26x) - likely FFI call overhead
- Complex indicators (RSI, MFI, ADX) show true algorithmic advantage
